Help & FAQs

How do I know if I’m eligible?

Use the Eligibility checker above and review the details on each scheme. Always verify on the official portal before applying.

Where do I apply?

Click “Apply Now” on the scheme card or result to visit the official portal. Never share OTPs or passwords with anyone.

Are these schemes updated?

We regularly refresh data, but official portals are the final authority for dates, documents, and criteria.
